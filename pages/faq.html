Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main goal of this website?

Our goal is to make learning easier by converting traditional PDF textbooks into searchable, and easy-to-read HTML and CSS pages, with a focus on Student Learning Outcomes (SLOs).

Why HTML/CSS instead of PDFs?

HTML and CSS pages are more accessible and easier to navigate. They allow for a better reading experience on various devices and can be easily updated and shared.

How can I find a specific chapter?

We plan to organize all content by subject and chapter. Soon, we will also add a search bar to help you find exactly what you're looking for.

Is the content free to use?

Yes, all the content on this website is completely free for students to use for their studies.
