About the Author

Yaseen Rehan

Yaseen Rehan is a 16-year-old student passionate about making education more accessible. As a degree college student himself, he understands the challenges of finding clear and concise study materials. He created "Our Books" to provide a platform where students can find all their chapters in an organized, easy-to-read format.

His goal is to transform the way students study by converting complex PDF books into simple, well-structured HTML pages that focus on key learning objectives.
